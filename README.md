# Clinical Hours Ledger

A free, browser-based tracker for LMSW-to-LCSW supervised clinical hours in New York State — with a built-in client roster, session logging, and progress dashboard.

🔗 **[Open the tool](https://williking16.github.io/clinical-hours-ledger)**

---

## Features

- **Progress dashboard** — tracks your hours and time accrued in real time against New York's LCSW targets
- **Client roster** — store each client's code, age, modality, diagnosis/presenting concern, and notes once; select them from a dropdown when logging sessions
- **Client profiles** — view all sessions, total hours, and details for any client in one place
- **Session logging** — log direct client contact and clinical supervision sessions in seconds
- **Pace projection** — estimates your completion timeline based on your current logging pace
- **Export** — download your data as a CSV (for spreadsheets) or JSON (for backup and restore)

---

## Privacy

All data saves to **your own browser** via `localStorage`. Nothing is sent to any server, no account is required, and no one else can see your entries — every person who opens this tool has their own completely private, isolated copy.

Because data lives in your browser, it will be lost if you clear your browser cache. **Use the Settings & Backup tab to download a backup regularly.**

> ⚠️ Do not enter any identifying client information. Use codes or initials only. This tool is not a HIPAA-secured system.

---

## How to use

1. **Visit the link above** — no installation or sign-in needed
2. **Set your start date** — go to Settings & Backup and enter your LMSW date (first time only)
3. **Add your clients** — go to the Clients tab and add each client with their code, age, modality, and any notes
4. **Log sessions** — use the Log Session tab, pick a client from the dropdown, and the details auto-fill
5. **View a client profile** — click any client card in the Clients tab, or click "Profile" on any session row in History
6. **Back up your data** — download a `.json` backup file periodically from Settings & Backup

## New York LCSW requirements (summary)

| Requirement | Target |
|---|---|
| Direct client contact hours | 2,000 hours |
| Clinical supervision hours | 100 hours |
| Experience window | 36 – 72 months |

Hours must be in **diagnosis, psychotherapy, or assessment-based treatment planning**. Supervision must be in-person, individual or group, by a NY-licensed LCSW, psychologist, or psychiatrist. Always confirm current requirements at [op.nysed.gov](https://www.op.nysed.gov/professions/licensed-clinical-social-worker/license-requirements).
